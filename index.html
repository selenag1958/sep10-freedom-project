Context

The process of architecture is done by an architect and their tools. An architect is a person who goes through the whole process of planning and construction of a building. They are able to do this through their tools which some have been used for years, while others are more modern and new, and many new ones are to come.This page will show the past present and possible future technology in the field of architecture.
